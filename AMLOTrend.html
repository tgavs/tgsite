AMLO twitter trend by Tomás Gaviño

How does the twitter favs and retweets look like for Andres Manuel López Obrador twitter account?

For this analysis I used python to retrive AMLO´s tweets statistics from 01-jan-2016 to 29-aug-2019.
For each tweet I grabed the number of favs and performed a moving average of 10 periods; the same procedure was made for the number of retweets.
We observe that the peak of interactions occurred on july 2nd, 2018 whith more than 46K favs, the date that he won the mexican presidential elections. However, from september 2018, to september 2019, he is averaging around 11K - 12K favs per tweet.One day ahead of his first presidendial report it will be very interesting to see if the trend changes.
